A little bit about me.

I'm from Mayesville, South Carolina and I'm open to the possibilities of relocating. My journey into tech started with a degree in business and over 10 years of customer service experience. Along my journey through life, I realized how much I wanted a change in my job and where my career was heading. I started to learn how to code and with that, awakened my passion for design. I was hooked and wanted to learn so much more through ux design, visual design, front-end development and more.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to ui/ux designer with front-end development skills. I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
